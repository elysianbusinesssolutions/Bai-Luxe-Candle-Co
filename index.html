Discover the essence of Bai Luxe Candle Co.

Luxury, culture, and calm, poured by hand.

Unveil the story behind our handcrafted luxury scented candles. Founded in Phoenix, Arizona in 2022, each candle is hand-poured with a luxurious blend of premium waxes and fine fragrances, evoking the richness of island sunsets, tropical breezes, and soulful storytelling. Bai Luxe transforms everyday moments into indulgent rituals of beauty and calm.

Our collection celebrates individuality, offering custom scents, labels, and designs that reflect your style and memories. From our Caribbean-inspired roots to our meticulous creative process, every Bai Luxe creation is designed to illuminate your world with sophistication, culture, and timeless elegance.
