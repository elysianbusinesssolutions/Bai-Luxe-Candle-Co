Our Story

Rooted in
Island Soul

Founded in Phoenix, Arizona in 2022 by C. Ferguson, Bai Luxe Candle Co. draws inspiration from the vibrant spirit of the Caribbean and the deep warmth of Jamaican heritage.

Each candle is hand-poured with a luxurious blend of premium para-soy waxes and fine fragrances — evoking island sunsets, tropical breezes, and soulful storytelling. Bai Luxe transforms everyday moments into indulgent rituals of beauty and calm.

Our collection celebrates individuality — offering custom scents, labels, and designs that reflect your style and memories. Every Bai Luxe creation is designed to illuminate your world with sophistication, culture, and timeless elegance.
