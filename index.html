Our Story

Crafted with Soul

Founded in Phoenix, Arizona in 2022, Bai Luxe Candle Co. was born from a love of fragrance, culture, and the art of creating something beautiful by hand.

Each candle is hand-poured with a luxurious blend of premium para-soy waxes and fine fragrances — designed to transform everyday moments into something worth remembering.

We celebrate individuality through custom scents and designs, making every Bai Luxe creation as unique as the person burning it.
